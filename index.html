The Journey

Roadmap

The Apex universe expands with every milestone.

Phase I

The Summoning

Collection launch on OpenSea. 2,222 warriors minted. The Apex trait rarity scores are published. Every warrior is ranked, the rarest rise to the top. Secondary market trading opens and the hunt for the most powerful Apex begins.

Phase II

The Awakening

3D print-ready files, allowing every holder to bring their Primordial into the physical world. The centerpiece of this evolution is the launch of $MUD, our official ecosystem token.

Phase III

The Convergence

Cross-faction merging mechanic. Evolve your warrior by combining trait fragments. Elemental Staking (Non-Custodial): Simply by resting your clay in your wallet, you earn $MUD token.

Phase IV

The Apex Realm

Playable Apex universe goes live. Warriors become playable characters. Exclusive alpha access for all 2,222 original holders.
